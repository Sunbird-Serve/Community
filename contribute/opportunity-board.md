# Opportunity Board

Every SERVE deployment creates opportunities for the community to contribute.

The current focus is **Telangana Program Readiness,** preparing the platform, training, and enablement needed for colleges and coordinators to begin using SERVE effectively.

Each contribution, however small, helps strengthen reusable assets that benefit future SERVE deployments across states and organizations.

***

### Current Focus

The Telangana rollout requires community contributions across documentation, training, testing, and enablement.

The work created here won't just support Telangana, it will become part of the shared SERVE ecosystem for every future implementation.
